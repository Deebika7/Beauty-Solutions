Eyeshadow


Now that you’ve prepped your skin with foundation and concealer, it’s time to focus on playing up your features. Eyeshadow is a good way to accentuate your natural eye shape. Invest in a basic eyeshadow palette with the right mix of light and dark shades. Neutral shadow shades really help open up and brighten the eyes; adding a touch of medium-to-darker hues may help add definition
